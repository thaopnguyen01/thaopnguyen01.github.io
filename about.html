About

A short biography, a career arc, and a few ways to get in touch.

Thao Nguyen

I build systems that solve hard problems at scale — end to end, from the first line of code to the engineering specification that scales them to production.

My background spans biology, frontline healthcare, regulatory compliance, operations, and AI/ML implementation — a combination that lets me move fluidly between the science of a problem, the regulatory framework around it, the people and processes that operationalize it, and the technical architecture that solves it at scale.

I turn fragmented, high-volume data into structured intelligence that helps organizations detect risk, automate decisions, and act with confidence. I'm drawn to consequential problems — where precision matters, the stakes are real, and better systems create measurable impact at scale. That's what gets me out of bed in the morning, whether the domain is public health, RegTech, consumer safety, or environmental risk.

Outside of work, I cook a lot, travel slowly when I can, and keep a long list of books I'm always one weekend away from finishing.
