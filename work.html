Professional Projects

End-to-End Regulatory Intelligence & Risk Detection Pipeline

Global marketplace clients with tens of millions of listings had no scalable way to detect regulatory violations — analysts were manually hunting through data using keyword filters, missing emerging risk patterns, and unable to keep pace with volume.

AI/ML NLP Risk Scoring RegTech Databricks Python

What I built: An end-to-end system spanning automated regulatory alert ingestion, multilingual translation, algorithmic similarity matching, likelihood × impact risk scoring, automated classification, and explainable decision insights — processing millions of data points weekly. Analyst workflow transformed from manual data hunting to a curated pre-scored queue where risk is already identified, classified, and explained.

Algorithmic Similarity Matching Engine

Known risk profiles keep re-emerging in modified forms — new merchant accounts, slightly altered product names, reformulated ingredients. Keyword-based detection misses these entirely. ML-based approaches require training data that doesn't exist for new categories.

NLP Entity Matching Public Health RegTech Python

What I built: An algorithmic similarity pipeline — direct matching, token overlap, and Levenshtein distance — that detects re-emerging risk patterns and surfaces similar entities without manual keyword curation or model training. Fully explainable, adaptive across any content category, and generalizes immediately to new product types.
